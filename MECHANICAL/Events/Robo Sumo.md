# Robo Sumo

Enter the ring where strategy meets raw power! In this circular arena battle, your bot's mission is simple: push your opponent out before they push you. Design a heavyweight warrior within strict specs, choose your control style—autonomous brain or RC precision—and dominate the 5-minute showdown. No blades, no tricks, just pure pushing force and tactical positioning. One bot enters, one bot wins. Will yours hold the ground or claim the ring? Build tough, drive smart, and fight for supremacy!

## Rules and Regulations

- Objective: Push your opponent out of the circular arena to win
- Match Duration: 5 minutes per round (subject to adjustment)
- Bot Specifications:
  - Max 30×30×30 cm
  - Max 5 kg (±5%)
  - 12V limit
  - Autonomous or RC (no mode switching mid-match)
  - Mandatory pre-match inspection
- Combat Rules:
  - No damaging tools (blades, hooks, adhesives, entanglement devices)
  - No touching bot once match starts
  - One bot per team in ring
  - Non-functional bot >10 seconds requires repair within allotted time (penalties apply)
- Victory Condition: Force opponent out of the arena
