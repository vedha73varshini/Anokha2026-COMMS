## Objectives
- To introduce the fundamentals of robotics and automation
- Covering theory, core concepts, and different types of robots.
- To provide hands-on experience in mastering the ESP32 by 
- Interfacing ESP32 with essential components like motors, sensors, and actuators for practical robotics.
  
## Outcomes
- Participants will gain a foundational understanding of modern automated systems and industry trends in robotics.
- Attendees will be able to design, program, and implement a functional robotic solution, such as a robocar, using the ESP32 in a team-based project.
