# POWER QUEST: The EEE Escape Challenge
A two-level escape-room themed technical competition where participants must restore  power by solving Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) tasks. They identify correct circuits, solve equivalent networks, perform KCL/KVL, strip wires safely, and build working circuits to unlock final codes.

## Rules and Regulations
- Participation is team-based with 2-3 teams per team; all members must work together.
- Tasks must be completed level-wise; skipping levels is not permitted.
- Each level is time-bound; failure to complete within time leads to elimination.
- Only the provided components may be used.
- Damaging components, unsafe handling, or malpractice will result in disqualification.
- Judges’ and coordinators’ decisions are final and binding.
