# Power Quest
A two-level escape-room themed technical competition where participants must restore  power by solving Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) tasks. They identify correct circuits, solve equivalent networks, perform KCL/KVL, strip wires safely, and build working circuits to unlock final codes.
