#  CAMPUS QUEST – The Ultimate Treasure Hunt 

A fun-filled, non-technical treasure hunt conducted inside the college campus. Teams
of four will solve clues, complete challenges, and navigate through multiple levels to reach the final treasure. The event promotes collaboration, quick thinking, and excitement among students from all years.

## Rules and Regulations

- Participation is team-based with 4 members per team; all members must stay together.
- Levels must be completed in order; skipping is not allowed.
- Each level is time-bound; incomplete teams will be eliminated.
- Phones may be used only for clues and QR scanning.
- Do not tamper with clues or QR codes.
- Organizers’ decisions are final.
