# Shipwreck

A dramatic life-or-death scenario: a ship is sinking, and there is only one life jacket remaining. The judge acts as the ship's captain who must decide who receives the last life jacket and survives.

## Rules and Regulations

- Each participant will be assigned/selects a famous personality (historical figure, celebrity, fictional character, leader, scientist, artist, etc.) and must fully embody them
- Adopt character's mannerisms, speech, and perspective
  - Argue why your character deserves the life jacket based on their value to humanity/society
- No preparationâ€”improvise all arguments on the spot
- Deliver rebuttals against other participants while defending your character's case
- Judging will be based on improvisation, character authenticity, persuasive speaking, quick wit, rebuttal strength, and stage presence
- Number of rounds decided by judge on event day
- Judge's decisions are final
