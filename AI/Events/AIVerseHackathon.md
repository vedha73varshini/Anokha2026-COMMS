## Rules and Regulations

- **Team size:** 2–4 members per team. Registration fee non-refundable.
- **Problem statement:** Each team may submit for **only one** problem statement/domain.
- **Original work only:** Submissions must be original and created for this hackathon. No prior projects, assignments, or reused/partially developed work.
- **Submission deadline:** All final submissions by **December 25, 2025**.
- **Mandatory submission items:**
  - Project presentation (PPT) with overview, architecture, and future scope.  
  - Public GitHub repository with complete codebase.  
  - Demo video (max 4 minutes).
- **Demo video rules:**
  - Maximum duration: **4 minutes**.  
  - Show at least **30%** of implemented features.  
  - Provide full solution walkthrough.  
  - Submit via **Google Drive or YouTube link**.  
  - Clearly showcase all functional components.
- **Prototype links:**
  - Web solutions: Deployed prototype link **optional but encouraged**.  
  - AIoT/Hardware: **Wokwi simulation link mandatory** for Phase 1.  
  - Physical hardware demo required **only if shortlisted** for Grand Finale.

- **Shortlisting:**
  - Top **10 teams per domain** (30 total) shortlisted on **December 27**.  
  - Only shortlisted teams proceed to on-campus Grand Finale on **January 8**.

- **For shortlisted teams:**
  - Improve/refine prototype from **Dec 27–Jan 7** under mentor guidance.  
  - Mandatory on-campus presence on **January 8**.  
  - Final pitch: **10-minute presentation + 5-minute Q&A**.

- **Certificates and prizes:**
  - All teams that submit a prototype receive **e-certificates of participation**.  
  - Winners, 1st and 2nd runners-up in each domain receive **special recognition certificates, prize money, and mementos**.
