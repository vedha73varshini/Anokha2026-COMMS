# Mime Competition

A themed mime competition where teams depict “Artificial Feelings: A New Era – AI & Human Life” through expressive, non-verbal performance. Participants explore both the positive and negative impacts of AI on human emotions, creativity, privacy, and daily life using only gestures and movement.

## Rules and Regulations

- Each team must consist of 7–10 members.
- Face painting is not allowed; masks or bare face performances are permitted.
- Performance duration must be between 7–10 minutes per team.
- Teams must bring their own performance-related items and props.
- The competition will be evaluated by the designated judges, and their decision will be final.
