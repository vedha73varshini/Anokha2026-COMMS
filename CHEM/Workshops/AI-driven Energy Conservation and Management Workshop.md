## AI-driven Energy Conservation and Management 
The objective of this workshop is to equip participants with a strong understanding of the fundamental principles of industrial energy management, including how energy is consumed, monitored, and controlled across various sectors.

## Speaker

- **Dr J Nagesh Kumar** <br>
Director at Centre for Energy, Environment and Productivity (CEEP) , Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India
- **Sakthi Aadharsh Azhagar**<br>
Energy Analyst at Centre for Energy Environment and Productivity , Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.


## Objectives
- Bridge the gap between traditional energy auditing methods and modern advancements
- Demonstrate how Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) can significantly improve the accuracy, efficiency, and effectiveness of energy conservation practices
- Highlight how integrating AI/ML with conventional systems can enable predictive insights, anomaly detection, real-time optimisation, and intelligent decision-making, ultimately reducing operational costs and enhancing sustainability in industrial environments.
  
## Outcomes
- Clearly understand the basic ideas behind energy conservation and management.
- Be able to spot where energy is being wasted and estimate how big the losses are.
- Learn how AI/ML can make energy saving easier by helping predict problems and suggest smarter decisions.
- Get familiar with important ISO standards and common industrial practices.
