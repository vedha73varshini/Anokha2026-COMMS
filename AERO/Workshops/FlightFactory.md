
## Flight Factory
A two-day hands-on experiential learning workshop for passionate students to gain 
practical knowledge in aerodynamics, 3D design, fabrication, and piloting of fixed-wing RC 
aircraft. This isn't just a workshop - IT'S YOUR CHANCE TO CARVE AND FLY A FULLY FUNCTIONAL RC 
PLANE, that too FROM SCRATCH!!

## Objectives
- To provide students with hands-on practical knowledge in aeromodelling and RC aircraft 
technology
- Enabling students to design, fabricate, and pilot a fixed-wing RC aircraft
  
## Outcomes
- Design fixed-wing aircraft from aerodynamic and aeromodelling first principles.
- Fabricate airframes using reliable techniques.
- Wire and integrate flight control systems and RC electronics.
- Execute pre-flight checks and safety protocols.
- Pilot your creation under professional supervision.
