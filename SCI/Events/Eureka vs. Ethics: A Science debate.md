# Eureka vs. Ethics: A Science debate

This debate event offers students a platform to engage with contemporary scientific and technological issues through structured arguments, audience interaction, and faculty evaluation, fostering spontaneity, critical thinking, and effective communication.

## Rules and Regulations

- Participation is team-based, with **4 members per team**.
- The event will be conducted over **one day**, featuring **4 debate sessions**.
- Each debate session will have a **maximum duration of 1 hour**.
- Teams must strictly follow the **prescribed debate format**.
- **Surprise topics** will be revealed only at the start of the session.
- Participants are expected to maintain **respectful and professional conduct** at all times.
- The **faculty panel’s decision** will be final and binding.

## Topic Pool

1. Will AI win the next Nobel Prize?
2. The Evolving Landscape of Data: Statistics or Machine Learning
3. The Role of R&D in India’s Growth Story
4. Genetically Modified Foods: Safe and Necessary?
5. The Impact of Cinema on Public Understanding of Science
6. Surprise Debate

_Surprise Round: The topic will be disclosed right as the session begins.*_
