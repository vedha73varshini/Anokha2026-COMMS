

## Pot Painting Workshop


## Speaker

- **Artist Kannan**
Artist Kannan is a faculty member at the Amrita School of Architecture with over 25 years of experience teaching art in architecture schools and more than 15 years of professional practice. His work lies at the intersection of art, craft, and architecture, with a strong emphasis on hands on learning, material exploration, and the act of making. With deep expertise in pottery and tactile creative processes, he brings a practice driven approach that encourages students to learn through direct engagement with materials.
