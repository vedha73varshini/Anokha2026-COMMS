# RoboBlaze

Build your custom wireless bot, conquer the precision Task Race, then dominate in Capture the Flag! Navigate tight tracks, deploy smart strategies, and outmaneuver rivals in this ultimate test of engineering and control. 30×30×30 cm of pure competition—are you ready to battle?

## Rules and Regulations

- Bot Specs: Manual wireless wheeled robots only (no ready-made RC cars) • Max 30×30×30 cm (±10%) • 20V max, on-board power • Simple hook/scoop allowed • No ramming/flipping/destructive mechanisms
- Competition Format: Track width 40–60 cm • Round 1 (Task Race) → qualifiers advance to Round 2 (Capture the Flag)
- No touching bot during runs
- Exiting track or staying immobile >7 sec will lead to penalty restart/reset
- Positional blocking allowed during round 2 but intentional ramming/damage will lead to disqualification
- All bots will be inspected pre-event
- Judges' decisions are final and binding
