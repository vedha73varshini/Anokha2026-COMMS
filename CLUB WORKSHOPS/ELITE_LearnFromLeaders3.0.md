

## Learn from Leaders 3.0

“Learn from Leaders” is the flagship event of ELITE club featuring inspiring workshops and
interactive sessions with accomplished professionals who share their journeys, challenges, and
lessons learned along the way. This includes a comprehensive Q&A and interactive discussion
segment where participants can engage directly with industry leaders and gain valuable insights.


## Speaker

1. **Mr. Jaljith Narayanan**
- **General Manager & Head - Human Resources, Nissan Digital India LLP**

Mr. Jaljith Narayanan is a seasoned Human Resources leader with over two decades of experience in the technology and automotive sectors. Currently serving as the General Manager and Head of HR at Nissan Digital India, he spearheads strategic initiatives in talent acquisition, organizational development, and digital upscaling to support the company’s automotive technology goals. Prior to his leadership at Nissan, Mr. Narayanan held key HR positions at UST Global, where he played a pivotal role in talent management and corporate strategy. An alumnus of Rajagiri College of Social Sciences with a Master’s in Personnel Management & Industrial Relations (PM&IR), he is widely recognized for his expertise in navigating post-pandemic work models, fostering work-life integration, and driving employee engagement in hybrid environments. A thought leader in the industry, Mr. Narayanan is frequently invited to speak on the evolving landscape of recruitment and the future of work in the digital era.

2. **Ms. Swathi Sri**
- **Co-Founder & COO - A Toddler Thing (Aikya Clothing Private Limited)**
  
Ms. Swathi Sri is the Co-Founder and Chief Operating Officer of A Toddler Thing, a pioneer in sustainable baby care products based in Coimbatore. An engineer turned ’mom-preneur,’ she established the brand in 2017 with a vision to revolutionize modern parenting through eco-friendly, GOTS-certified organic solutions. Under her leadership, the company has scaled significantly, securing funding from investors like Mumbai Angels and gaining national recognition, including a feature on Shark Tank India Season 3. Ms. Swathi is passionate about sustainable living and has demonstrated exceptional leadership in navigating the D2C (Direct-to-Consumer) landscape.

## Objective
- Inspire and empower participants by exposing them to real-life leadership journeys that spark motivation and clarity in their personal and career paths. 
- The event also encourages interactive engagement through Q&A and discussions, boosting confidence while fostering meaningful networking with leaders, mentors, and peers.
  
## Outcomes
- Instill a sense of purpose and positive societal contribution among participants by emphasizing ethical practices and social responsibility
- Enhance participants’ understanding of innovation, intellectual property, and technology leadership through real world industry perspectives.
