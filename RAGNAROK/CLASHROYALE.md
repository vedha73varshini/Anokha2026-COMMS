# RAGNAROK : CLASH ROYALE 

Clash Royale is a real-time, strategy-based multiplayer mobile game that combines card mechanics, tower defense, and fast-paced decision-making. As part of Ragnarök, the Clash Royale tournament delivers a competitive esports environment where players engage in structured one-on-one battles under standardized tournament conditions. The event tests participants’ strategic thinking, deck-building skills, reaction time, and adaptability under pressure.

## Prize Money

- Second Prize - Rs. 4,500/-
- Third Prize - Rs. 2,500/-

## Challenge:
The tournament will begin with approximately 140 participants (expected) capped at 150 competing in an in-game Clash Royale Mega Draft tournament, where all players queue simultaneously within a shared participant pool, and matchmaking functions similarly to standard online battles but is restricted to registered tournament players. Each match typically lasts 2–5 minutes, with matchmaking handled automatically by the game, allowing players to continuously queue and play matches until the tournament timer expires.1 Day Tournament where in the first hour we select top 100, top 50 in the second hour, top 25 in the third hour with which top 3 final winners will be determined based on overall tournament standings


## Rules and Regulations
All events under Ragnarök will strictly adhere to the following rules and regulations:
- General Rules:
  - The event is open only to paid participants.
  - Participants must register before the deadline.
  - On-spot registrations are subject to availability.
  - Participants must report at least 15 minutes before their scheduled match.
  - Organizers’ and referees’ decisions are final and binding.
- Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Rules
  - Participants must bring their own mobile devices and chargers.
  - Devices must be in proper working condition before reporting.
  - Internet access will be provided only if specified; otherwise, players must ensure connectivity.
  - Participants are responsible for the safety of their devices.
  - Organizers are not responsible for damage, loss, or malfunction of personal devices.
- Code of Conduct
  - Professional and sportsmanlike behavior is mandatory.
  - Harassment, toxicity, abuse, or offensive language will result in immediate disqualification.
  - Use of emulators, mods, cheats, exploits, or third-party tools is strictly prohibited.
  - Participants must comply with instructions from referees and organizers at all times.
- Game Rules:
  - Matches will be played on the latest stable version of Clash Royale.
  - Tournament Standard / Friendly Battle settings will be used.
  - Deck changes are allowed between matches, not during an ongoing match.
  - Any in-game bugs or technical issues must be reported immediately.
  - Intentional exploitation of glitches will lead to forfeiture or disqualification.
- Match Flow & Punctuality
  - Failure to join a match within 10 minutes of the scheduled time will result in automatic forfeit.
  - In case of disconnection, match restart or result validation will be decided by referees.
