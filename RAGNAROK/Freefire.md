# RAGNAROK - Free Fire

Free Fire is a fast-paced multiplayer battle royale mobile game that emphasizes survival, teamwork, strategy, and mechanical skills. As part of Ragnarok, the Free Fire tournament provides a competitive esports platform where players engage in structured matches under standardized tournament rules. The event challenges participants’ tactical decision-making, reflexes, coordination, and adaptability in high-pressure combat scenarios.

## Tournament Format

- The tournament will commence with approximately 30–35 teams, with a maximum cap of 48 teams, competing in custom Free Fire room matches exclusively organized for registered participants.
- Game Mode: Classic Clash Squad (5 rounds)
- All matches will be conducted with character skills and special weapon attributes disabled. 
- Unlimited ammo will be enabled, and in-game currency will be fixed at 10,000 for weapon purchases and all weapons will be enabled with no upgrades.
- 48 teams, 4 groups 12, 12, 12, 12 each.
- Round 1: Each group, 6 matches are played in parallel. 6 teams in each group advance to round 2.
- Round 2: Each group, 3 matches are played in parallel. 3 teams in each group advance to round 3.
- Round 3: 2 groups combine to make one group, essentially making two groups of 6 teams each. 3 matches are played in parallel in each group. 3 teams advance to round 4 in each group.
- Round 4: Remaining 2 groups are combined to form a pool of 6 teams. 3 matches are played in parallel. 3 teams advance to Round 5.
- Round 5: Each team plays against each other. The team with the lowest score will get their place accordingly. The remaining teams will fight for the remaining places.
- Each match will last approximately 10–15 minutes, with total tournament duration of 2 hrs and 
with a buffer period of 1 hr.

## Rules and Regulations

1. General Rules
  - Open only to paid and registered participants.
  - Complete registration before the deadline; on-spot subject to availability.
  - Teams may participate in multiple events if timings don't clash.
  - Report at the venue at least 15 minutes before the match.
  - Organizers' and referees' decisions are final and binding.

2. Bring Your Own PC (BYOP) Rules
  - Bring your own PC/laptop, peripherals (Mouse, Headset, Keyboard, NO MONITORS), power adapters, and cables.
  - Systems must be fully functional.
  - Power sockets and LAN access will be shared.
  - Participants are solely responsible for equipment safety and security.
  - Organizers are not responsible for damage, loss, theft, or malfunction of personal equipment.

3. Code of Conduct
  - Maintain professional and sportsmanlike behavior.
  - Harassment, abusive language, toxicity, discrimination, or unsportsmanlike conduct results in immediate disqualification.
  - Cheating, hacking, use of exploits, macros, scripts, or unauthorized third-party software is strictly prohibited.
  - Comply with all instructions from referees and event staff.

4. Game Rules & Settings
  - Game mode: Clash Squad
  - Matches will be played on the latest version of Free Fire.
  - Only custom room matches created by organizers will be used.
  - Any technical issues or in-game bugs must be reported immediately.
  - Intentional exploitation of glitches will result in forfeiture or disqualification.
  - Any additional changes and the tournament structure will informed at the venue

5. Match Flow & Punctuality:
   - Failure to join a match within 10 minutes of the scheduled start time will result in automatic forfeit.
   - In case of disconnection, match continuation or result validation will be decided by referees based on fairness and match status
